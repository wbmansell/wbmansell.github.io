Our services...

Web Design

Our website designs are created with the end-user in mind. We do not design websites that rely on the assumption that your viewers all have high-speed internet connections. We will assist you from design concept to development, through to posting your new website to the web space provider (host) of your choice. Check out our Featured website work below.

Photo Restoration

You have old photos that you or your grandparents have hung on to that are sitting in a shoebox somewhere or a photo album tucked away & forgotten. As those photos age, they've possibly yellowed & torn with multiple scratches and other miscellaneous marks on them. Once scanned, we'll do our best to restore the photo's original black & white or colour quality.

Video Conversion

You don't want to lose all those memories you have stored away on 8mm film, VHS tape or your many photo albums? In most cases we're able to transfer 8mm film, VHS tape & photos to DVD format. As an added bonus, we can enhance your existing video collection & jazz it up some, by adding all sorts of extras such as titling, transitions & background music.

Who we are...

Our primary business is providing website creation services to small & medium-sized companies and individuals looking to develop a presence on the internet. While we don't dabble with sites such as eCommerce shopping or database-driven catalogs, we can still help you discover how you can become visible on the internet through an established website.

We will assist you throughout the website lifecycle... from design concept, to web page development, through to posting your new website to the web space provider of your choice.

And after your website is live, you can either choose to continue to utilize our services to support & maintain your new website, or look after the website yourself.

Fees & Hourly Rate

We don't employ any complicated formulae to calculate what you'll be charged. Our rate for all work completed is charged at the hourly rate of $60/hour, in half-hour increments.

NOTE: For volunteer / non-profit organizations, our hourly rate drops to $45/hour.
